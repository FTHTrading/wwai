# Payer / Client Demo Script

**Audience:** payer, sponsor, restaurant owner, hotel GM, fleet manager, venue ops.
**Goal:** end with a saved proposal and a registration in `/admin`.
**Time:** 10 minutes if everything loads.

## Open

> "TROPTIONS is the operating system for an event city. WWAI is what your guests touch.
> It is independent — not affiliated with any league, team, hotel, rideshare or restaurant brand
> unless we sign that paper separately. Everything you see today is demo data. The structure is real;
> the integrations get switched on per customer."

## Walkthrough

Use **`/client-demo`** as the rail. It links to every step in order. Read the talk track at each stop.

1. **`/`** — TROPTIONS + WWAI in two paragraphs.
2. **`/wwai`** — guest concierge. Pick a preset question, change the language.
3. **`/packages`** — point at the tier closest to their budget.
4. **`/register`** — show their business type form. Submit a demo entry.
5. **`/map`** + **`/safety-routes`** — outdoor map, hotel→seat→hotel demo route.
6. **`/proposals`** — configure their package live, hit **Save Demo Proposal**, then **Print Preview**.
7. **`/admin`** — show the submission they just made and the proposal queued.
8. **`/analytics`** — explain that every metric is labeled Demo until real data is hooked.
9. **`/billing`** — show invoicing. Manual works in demo. Square / Stripe land when keys land.
10. **`/settings/integrations`** — single screen showing what is configured and what isn\u2019t.
11. **`/launch`** — the production checklist you sign off on together.

## Language guardrails

| Use | Avoid |
|---|---|
| safety-informed | guaranteed safe |
| demo route | official route |
| operator-reviewed in production | police-approved |
| recommended public corridor | secure route |
| verified partner | exclusive partner of |

## Emergency disclaimer (memorize)

> "Safety guidance is informational and demo-based. For emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately."

## Close

> "If we sign today, my next step is a kickoff call to gather your data, your CRM, your payment processor, and the venues you care about. From there it is a checklist — that\u2019s what `/launch` is for."
